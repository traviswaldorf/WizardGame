# Spell Archetype System

## The 10 Spell Archetypes

Every spell effect in the game falls into one of 10 mechanical archetypes, grouped into 5 strategic categories.

### Aggro
| Archetype | Description                          | Example Effect              |
|-----------|--------------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Damage    | Deal direct damage to towers/players | Fireball: Deal X damage     |
| Destroy   | Remove a tower or permanent outright | Nuke: Destroy all towers on an element |

### Control
| Archetype | Description                          | Example Effect              |
|-----------|--------------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Counter   | Negate a spell or action             | Gust: Counter spell on your turn |
| Steal     | Take resources or elements from opponents | Attraction: Steal elements from other places |

### Search
| Archetype | Description                          | Example Effect              |
|-----------|--------------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Draw      | Gain additional cards or elements    | Combustion: Draw additional elements |

### Growth
| Archetype | Description                          | Example Effect              |
|-----------|--------------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Revive    | Recover or replay spent resources    | Ghost: Replay a spell you've already played |
| Discount  | Reduce costs of future actions       | Metal: Build tower for reduced cost |

### Defensive
| Archetype | Description                          | Example Effect              |
|-----------|--------------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Prevent   | Stop or reduce incoming damage       | Protection/Rock Wall: Protect tower for a turn |
| Block     | Shield or armor a target             | Metal Shield: Attacks do reduced damage |

### Unassigned
| Archetype | Description                          | Notes                       |
|-----------|--------------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Discard   | Force opponents to lose cards/elements | Needs strategic category assignment |
