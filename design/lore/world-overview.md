## Cosmology
The world is built on a foundation of four primal forces — Fire, Earth, Water, and Air — each representing a state of matter and a domain of energy. Where these forces overlap, secondary elements emerge (Metal, Plant, Ice, Electric). Deeper still, each element has a shadow side — a tertiary form that represents its darker, more extreme expression.

Beyond the elemental system, two augmenting forces exist outside the physical hierarchy:
- **Time and Space** — governing temporal and spatial dimensions
- **Light and Dark** — governing the expression of each element as its standard or shadow form

## The Old Names
Practitioners of each element are known by their Old Names — titles using the thematic -o postfix that connects them to their elemental heritage. These names carry power and identity.
