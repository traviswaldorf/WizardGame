## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
Growth and healing. Plant combines Earth's slow buildup with Water's resource flow to create a life-giving engine. Gathering resources, healing towers, and nurturing long-term advantage.

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Defensive (growth-oriented)
- **Cluster Memberships**: Generation/Expand, Protection/Explore

### Playstyle Summary
Plant players win through sustained resource gathering and healing. They keep their towers alive, gather diverse resources, and build an engine that's hard to dismantle because it constantly repairs itself.
