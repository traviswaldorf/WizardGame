## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
Metal combines Fire's aggression with Earth's defense — fortified offense. Focus on building resilient towers and equipping them with lasting protections. Metal is about permanence and structure.

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Defensive (with Aggressive secondary)
- **Cluster Memberships**: Protection/Explore, Aggressive/Exterminate

### Playstyle Summary
Metal players build fortified positions — towers with shields, reduced costs, and lasting protections. Can pivot between defensive tower-building and aggressive pushes using Metal's structural strength.
