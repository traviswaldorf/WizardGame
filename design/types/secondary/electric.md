## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
Electric combines Fire's aggression with Air's control into a fast, disruptive force. Paralyze opponents, deal burst damage, and control the pace. Electric is about sudden, shocking impact.

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Aggressive (with Control secondary)
- **Cluster Memberships**: Control/Exploit, Aggressive/Exterminate

### Playstyle Summary
Electric players win through burst damage and paralysis — stunning opponents at critical moments and following up with direct damage. Fast, precise, and punishing.
