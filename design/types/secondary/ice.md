## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
Ice combines Air's control with Water's flow into a freezing, tempo-denying force. Freeze players, freeze element sources, freeze spell sources. Ice is about slowing everyone else down while you maintain pace.

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Control (tempo)
- **Cluster Memberships**: Generation/Expand, Control/Exploit

### Playstyle Summary
Ice players win by controlling the tempo of the game — freezing opponents out of turns, locking down element and spell sources, and maintaining a speed advantage while others are frozen.

## 12. Interactions and Combos

### Composition
Air + Water = Ice. Air's control and denial combined with Water's flow creates a phase-change element that locks things in place. Mechanically, Air's counters become Ice's freezes, and Water's card flow becomes denial of opponent flow.
