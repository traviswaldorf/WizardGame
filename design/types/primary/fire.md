## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
Aggressive, damage-focused. Fast moving towards goal, burns quick. Focus on damaging other players and destroying towers. Fire wants to end the game before opponents can establish their engines.

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Aggressive
- **Cluster Memberships**: Aggressive/Exterminate

### Playstyle Summary
A Fire player wins by overwhelming opponents with direct damage and tower destruction before they can build up defenses. High tempo, high risk — burns through resources quickly but can close games fast.

## 10. Costs and Conditions

### Resource Philosophy
Fire spells are cheap but consumptive — they often require discarding cards or elements as additional costs to amplify their effects. This mirrors the "burns quick" philosophy.

### Typical Cost Patterns
- Low base cost + optional discard for amplification
- Sacrifice resources for burst damage
