## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
Defensive, slow buildup. Focus on building up a long-term engine through towers. Earth wants to outlast opponents through superior infrastructure and gravitational pull — drawing things together, making space.

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Defensive
- **Cluster Memberships**: Protection/Explore

### Playstyle Summary
An Earth player wins by building a fortress of towers over time, using protection spells to defend investments, and leveraging gravity mechanics to control board positioning. Slow to start, dominant in the late game.

## 3. MTG Correspondences

| MTG Color | Mechanic                    | Wizard Game Translation |
|-----------|-----------------------------|------------------------|
| White     | Damage Prevention           | Defense Boost          |
| White     | Protection                  | Protection             |
| White     | Toughness boosts            | Defense Boost          |
| Green     | Temporary Growth            | Temp Defense Boost     |

### Design Notes
Earth draws from both MTG White (protection, toughness) and Green (growth, temporary buffs). Diverges from MTG by tying defense to a gravity/mass metaphor rather than divine/nature.

## 5. Lore

### Old Name and Etymology
- **Old Name**: Kilo / Grano
- **Meaning/Origin**: Kilo from kilogram — the unit of mass. Grano from Latin "granum" (grain, earth material). Emphasizes Earth's connection to weight, gravity, and solidity.
