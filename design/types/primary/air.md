## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
Control-oriented. Counters and board control. Focus on controlling other player actions through board control, card control, and counters. Air doesn't win by building — it wins by preventing opponents from executing their plans.

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Control
- **Cluster Memberships**: Control/Exploit

### Playstyle Summary
An Air player wins by denying opponents their strategy — countering spells, forcing discards, clearing the board of status effects. Reactive and disruptive, Air creates openings by dismantling opponent plans.

Air maps closely to MTG Blue's control suite. The "clearing the board of status effects" concept (Tornado) is unique to this system — Air as a purifying/cleansing force.
