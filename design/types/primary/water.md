## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
Flow and acceleration. Quick flow of elements or spells. Focus on building up short-term options (spells, elements). Water is about having more cards, more choices, more tempo.

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Flow
- **Cluster Memberships**: Generation/Expand

### Playstyle Summary
A Water player wins by outpacing opponents with card and resource advantage. Draw more, play more, stay flexible. Water doesn't commit to a single strategy — it finds the best path through volume of options.

### Design Notes
Water takes the card draw mechanic from both MTG Blue and Green. Unlike Blue (which also controls), Water is purely about flow and acceleration — the control aspect lives in Air.
