## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
Focus, accuracy, and time manipulation. Crystal is about precision — replaying the right card at the right moment, undoing mistakes, and bending the flow of turns. Structure energy as lattice, bonds, and resonance.

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Control (tempo-based)
- **Cluster Memberships**: <!-- TBD -->

### Playstyle Summary
Crystal players win through perfect timing — replaying key spells, undoing opponent actions, and getting extra turns or actions. Not about raw power but about efficiency and precision.

## 3. MTG Correspondences

| MTG Color | Mechanic                          | Wizard Game Translation |
|-----------|-----------------------------------|------------------------|

### Design Notes
Crystal draws from MTG Blue's time/tempo mechanics but adds a unique "structural resonance" flavor. The lattice/bond metaphor is original.
