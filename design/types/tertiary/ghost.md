## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
The dark side of Plant/Life. Where Plant heals and grows, Ghost drains and decays. Stealing life from opponents' towers to recover your own, replaying spent spells from the "graveyard." Ghost thrives on what others have lost.

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Flow (parasitic)
- **Cluster Memberships**: <!-- TBD -->

### Playstyle Summary
Ghost players win through parasitism — draining life from opponent towers, replaying spent spells, and turning death into advantage. Every destroyed tower and discarded spell is fuel for Ghost's engine.

## 5. Lore

### Old Name and Etymology
- **Old Name**: Necro / Morto / Entro
- **Meaning/Origin**: Necro from Greek "nekros" (dead body, root of necromancy). Morto from Latin "mors/mortis" (death). Entro from "entropy" — the tendency toward disorder and decay.
