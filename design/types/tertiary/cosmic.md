## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
The grand-scale expression of Earth. Where Earth builds locally, Cosmic operates on a massive scale — meteors, gravity wells, tidal forces. Spread out something on the board (your towers, opponent pieces).

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Defensive
- **Cluster Memberships**: Protection/Explore

### Playstyle Summary
Cosmic combines Earth's defensive nature with board-wide spatial manipulation. Uses gravity to pull resources in and push opponents away, culminating in massive impact spells.

### Old Name and Etymology
