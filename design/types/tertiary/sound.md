## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
The aggressive expression of Air's control. Where Air denies through counters, Sound denies through overwhelming force — silencing opponents, detonating their hands, creating shockwaves that discard en masse.

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Control
- **Cluster Memberships**: Control/Exploit

### Playstyle Summary
Sound players win by stripping opponents of all options. Silence their communication, detonate their hands, and leave them with nothing to play. Control through annihilation of options rather than reactive countering.

## 5. Lore

### Old Name and Etymology
- **Old Name**: Sono / Stereo
- **Meaning/Origin**: Sono from Latin "sonus" (sound). Stereo from "stereophonic" — sound from multiple directions. Both represent the omnidirectional, unavoidable nature of sound.
