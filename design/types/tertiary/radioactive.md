## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
The extreme, nuclear expression of Fire. Where Fire burns, Radioactive annihilates. Mass destruction effects that damage everyone — including potentially the caster.

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Aggressive
- **Cluster Memberships**: Aggressive/Exterminate

### Playstyle Summary
Radioactive is the "scorched earth" strategy — powerful mass destruction effects that reshape the board but at high cost. Use when you're behind and need to reset, or when you're ahead and want to close out.

### Design Notes
Radioactive takes MTG Red's most destructive mechanics and amplifies them to board-wide effects. The "Nuke" super power concept is unique to this system.
