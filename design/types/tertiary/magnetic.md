## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
The information and attraction side of Electric. Where Electric shocks and paralyzes, Magnetic attracts, steals, and reveals. Intel and technology — knowing what opponents have and taking it.

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Control (information)
- **Cluster Memberships**: <!-- TBD -->

### Playstyle Summary
Magnetic players win through information advantage and resource theft. See all hands, steal the best elements, attract resources from across the board. Knowledge is power.

## 5. Lore

### Old Name and Etymology
- **Old Name**: Magneto / Polo
- **Meaning/Origin**: Magneto from "magnet" — the attractive force. Polo from "polar/poles" — the fundamental duality of magnetic fields. Both represent the invisible pull that draws resources and information.
