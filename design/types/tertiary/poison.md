## 2. Strategic Identity

### Philosophy
The dark side of Water. Where Water gives options, Poison takes them away. Decay over time, resource denial, and slow inevitable damage. If Water is about having more, Poison is about your opponents having less.

### Archetype Alignment
- **Primary Archetype**: Flow (inverted — negative flow)
- **Cluster Memberships**: <!-- TBD -->

### Playstyle Summary
Poison players win through attrition — applying persistent damage-over-time effects to towers and forcing opponents to discard elements. The game slows as opponents' options dry up.

## 5. Lore

### Old Name and Etymology
- **Old Name**: Toxo / Sporo
- **Meaning/Origin**: Toxo from Greek "toxikon" (poison). Sporo from Greek "spora" (spore, spreading contamination). Represents the corrupting, decaying transformation of Water.
