## Design Notes

- Super Powers are one per TYPE, not per type-pair
- They represent the deepest mastery of a single element
- Should feel like the culmination of understanding that type's science
- Mechanically: likely the most expensive, most impactful single play available
- Open question: are these fixed per type, or unlocked through discovery?
