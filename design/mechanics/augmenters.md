# Augmenters

Augmenter pairs exist outside the main 8-type hierarchy and modify or enhance other types.

## The Two Augmenter Pairs

### Time - Space
| Aspect | Domain               | Mechanical Theme               |
|--------|----------------------|--------------------------------|
| Time   | Temporal manipulation | Redo, undo, turn order, speed  |
| Space  | Spatial manipulation  | Board position, range, area    |

### Light - Dark
| Aspect | Domain                      | Mechanical Theme                          |
|--------|-----------------------------|-------------------------------------------|
| Light  | Standard type expression    | The "normal" form of each type            |
| Dark   | Tertiary/shadow expression  | The deeper, corrupted form (e.g., Poison = Dark Water) |

## Light/Dark Mapping

The tertiary types represent the "Dark" form of their parent types:

| Light Form | Dark Form   | Relationship             |
|------------|-------------|--------------------------|
| Fire       | Radioactive | Nuclear corruption of flame |
| Earth      | Cosmic      | Vast, alien scale of earth |
| Water      | Poison      | Corrupted, toxic water     |
| Air        | Sound       | Aggressive expression of air |
| Metal      | Crystal     | Refined, structured form   |
| Plant      | Ghost       | Death/decay of life        |
| Ice        | Heat        | Thermodynamic opposite     |
| Electric   | Magnetic    | Field-based expression     |
