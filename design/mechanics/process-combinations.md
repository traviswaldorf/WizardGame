# Process Combinations

> **Status**: Exploration
> **Purpose**: Catalog real physical/chemical/biological processes that occur at each type-pair intersection. These ground our spell combinations in science.
> **Feeds into**: [Combos and Synergies](combos-and-synergies.md)

A "process" is what happens when two types interact — not a weather event or terrain feature, but a transformation of matter or energy. Each process is a potential spell archetype.

Reminder: Our types are broader than their names suggest:
- **Fire** = plasma, energy, heat — not just flames
- **Earth** = solid matter, mineral, structure — not just dirt
- **Water** = liquid, flow, dissolution — not just H2O
- **Air** = gas, pressure, atmosphere — not just oxygen
- **Ice** = cold, crystallization, low energy — not just frozen water
- **Sound** = pressure waves, vibration, resonance — not just audible noise
- **Ghost** = death, decay, life-force — not just specters

## Primary × Primary

### Fire + Earth (→ Metal)

| Process | Description | Product / Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Smelting | Heat extracts metal from solid ore | → Metal (composition) |
| Vitrification | Extreme heat melts silica into glass | Solid → transparent solid transmutation |
| Kiln Firing | Heat hardens wet earth into ceramic | Soft solid → hard solid (permanent defense boost) |
| Calcination | Heat drives off volatiles from mineral | Purification — strip properties to base |
| Cooling / Solidification | Magma cools into ignite rock | Energy → solid (Fire becomes Earth) |
| Rapid Quench | Magma cooled instantly → obsidian | Extreme version of cooling — glassy, sharp result |

### Earth + Water (→ Plant)

|---|---|---|

| Dissolution | Water dissolves soluble solids | Earth consumed by Water — resource conversion |
