# Storm Inspirations — Weather, Terrain & Natural Phenomena

> **Status**: Exploration
> **Purpose**: Catalog real-world weather events, terrain features, and natural phenomena that emerge from type-pair interactions. These feed the [Storms](storms.md) system.
> **Distinction from [Process Combinations](process-combinations.md)**: Processes are transformations (smelting, erosion, conductivity). These are *environmental events* — things that happen TO the world, changing conditions for everyone.

---

## What Makes Something a Storm (vs a Process)?

- **Processes** change what something IS (solid → liquid, ore → metal)
- **Storms** change the CONDITIONS everything operates under (temperature, visibility, terrain stability)

A Blizzard doesn't transform anything — it makes everything colder, slower, harder to see. That's a Storm.
Vitrification turns sand into glass — that's a Process.
