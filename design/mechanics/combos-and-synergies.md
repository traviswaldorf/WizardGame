## Composition Relationships

How primary types combine to form secondary types (foundational synergies):

| Primary A | Primary B | Secondary | Mechanical Inheritance                    |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|-------------------------------------------|
| Fire      | Earth     | Metal     | Aggression + Defense = Fortified offense  |
| Earth     | Water     | Plant     | Stability + Flow = Growth engine          |
| Air       | Water     | Ice       | Control + Flow = Tempo denial             |
| Fire      | Air       | Electric  | Aggression + Control = Burst + Paralysis  |

## Design Principles

> "This needs to be tight (limited range of interactions) so these interact often enough."

- Keep the number of distinct synergies small but impactful
- Every synergy should fire frequently enough to be a viable build strategy
- Synergies should be discoverable through play, not require memorization
