# Type Combination Design — Master Table

> **Status**: Exploration
> **Purpose**: Single source of truth for all type-pair interactions, categorized by mechanical role.
> **Filtered views**: [Spells](type-combination-spells.md) · [Storms](type-combination-storms.md) · [Super Powers](type-combination-superpowers.md)

## Categories

| Category | What It Is | Scope | Quantity |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Spell** | A player-cast combination of two types. Transforms, damages, or creates. | Targeted / local | Many per pair |
| **Storm** | A natural environmental expression of a type combination. Board-wide conditions. | Board-wide / persistent | ~1 per pair |
| **Super Power** | The ultimate harnessed understanding of a single type's force. | One per type | 1 per type |
